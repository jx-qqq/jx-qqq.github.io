Project

Lab 1: Drinking vs Driving

Design Ideas

Alcohol consumption is common among American people, but different states have different drinking habits. In this project, I tried to associate excessive drinking to alcohol related traffic fatalities on a state level, and elaborated the difference with two typical states: Utah and Alaska.

Lab 2: Mapbox Design

Design Ideas

Water scarcity has long been an issue in the Middle East area. This map is focused on Cairo, Egypt, where Nile river runs lively through the city. The bright color of the river takes the metaphor that water is as precious as gold.
